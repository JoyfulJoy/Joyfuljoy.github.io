Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • name
  • contact information

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • reserve a table for you
  • contact you to confirm your attendance
  • verify the customer who says they have a reservation

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to provide your name, phone number, number of guests, date and time for reservation, we'll be unable to reserve a table for you.

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and only allow certain staff to access it.

We keep your information for 1 month at which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing all digits.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you would like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at info@haukai.co.nz, or 09-4014019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
